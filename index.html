ABOUT

Mrs. K Samgyeopsal is a renowned culinary destination for food lovers seeking the authentic Korean barbecue experience. Specializing in Samgyeopsal (grilled pork belly), Mrs. K offers a flavorful feast where guests can enjoy perfectly cooked, tender slices of pork paired with a variety of fresh sides, dips, and vegetables. The experience allows diners to grill their own meat at the table, creating a fun and interactive dining atmosphere. Known for its top-quality ingredients, friendly service, and inviting ambiance, Mrs. K Samgyeopsal is the go-to place for those craving the true taste of Korean BBQ, whether it's for a casual meal with friends or a special family gathering.
